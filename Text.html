    2. Extract text / copy text from images

    i2OCR is a free online Optical Character Recognition (OCR) service that allows users to convert scanned documents, PDFs, and images into editable text. The service supports a wide range of languages and output formats, including TXT, PDF, DOC, and HTML. The process of using i2OCR is fairly straightforward: you upload the image or document you want to convert, select the language and output format, and then the service will scan and extract the text from the image. i2OCR can be useful for people who need to digitize documents or extract text from images, as it saves them the time and effort of manually typing out the text. However, it's important to note that the accuracy of the OCR process can vary depending on the quality of the source image and the complexity of the text.
