      2. JustWatch is an online search engine that allows users to find where their favorite TV shows and movies are available for streaming, rental, or purchase. It was founded in 2014 by a group of film and tech enthusiasts in Berlin, Germany. To use JustWatch, a user can simply search for a movie or TV show title and the website will display a list of all the streaming and rental services where that content is available, such as Netflix, Amazon Prime, Hulu, and more. Users can filter their search results by location, price, and streaming quality. JustWatch is a useful tool for people who want to quickly and easily find out where they can watch a particular movie or TV show without having to search through multiple streaming services. It can save users time and money by helping them find the best streaming or rental options for the content they want to watch. JustWatch is available as a website and as mobile apps for iOS and Android.
